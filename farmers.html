Where it comes from

We buy the whole harvest.

Most craft brands buy a sack of roots from a trader and call it sourcing. We work the other end of the chain: we take the crop off the farmer's field — more than three hundred tonnes of it — and find a use for every part.

01 · FIELD

Independent farmers

Vetiver is bought directly from smallholders around Dindigul at a committed price, before harvest. No trader sits in the middle taking the margin.

02 · YARD

Cleaned and separated

Roots are washed, sorted by length and grade, and sun-dried. The finest, longest fibre is set aside — that's what a window hanging is made of.

03 · NOTHING WASTED

Every grade finds a home

Shorter fibre goes into cushions and quils. Buying the whole crop is what lets us pay for the whole crop.
