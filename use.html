How to use it

Hang it. Mist it. That's all.

There is no ritual to learn and nothing to replace. The roots do the work; water wakes them up.

STEP 01

Hang it where air moves

An entrance, a bedroom door, the pooja room, a car mirror, a desk. Airflow is what carries the aroma — a sealed cupboard will do nothing.

STEP 02

Mist it with water

A light spray, not a soak. The roots release their cool, earthy scent as the water evaporates. Repeat whenever the room needs it.

STEP 03

Let it breathe

Keep it out of standing sun and let air circulate. Cared for this way, a single window hanging stays fragrant for up to 36 months.
